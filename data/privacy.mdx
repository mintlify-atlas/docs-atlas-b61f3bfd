# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated**: July 8, 2025

This privacy policy explains how Novelty handles your personal information and data. Novelty is developed and maintained by 唐澤 克幸 (Katsuyuki Karasawa).

## Data Collection

Novelty is designed with privacy as a core principle.

### Information Automatically Collected

Novelty collects the following information locally on your device:

#### Reading History

- **What**: Novel Ncodes and episode numbers you've read
- **Where Stored**: Locally on your device only
- **Purpose**: To track your reading progress and provide "Continue Reading" functionality
- **External Transmission**: Never transmitted to external servers

#### Usage Data

- **What**: App feature usage, performance data, crash reports
- **Includes**: Device type, OS version, unique device identifiers (anonymized)
- **Purpose**: App quality improvement and troubleshooting
- **Transmission**: May be transmitted for debugging purposes

<Note>
Usage data does NOT include personal information like your name, address, or email. Device identifiers are anonymized and cannot be linked to you personally.
</Note>

### External Services

Novelty uses the following external services:

#### 小説家になろう API

- **Purpose**: Fetch novel information, rankings, and episode content
- **Data Transmitted**: Your IP address and API requests
- **Privacy Policy**: [小説家になろうグループ Personal Information Protection Policy](https://syosetu.com/site/privacy/)

<Info>
When you use Novelty to fetch novels, rankings, or episodes, your requests go directly to 小説家になろう's servers. Novelty does not act as an intermediary or log these requests.
</Info>

Information collected by Novelty is used for:

### Service Provision and Maintenance

### Service Improvement

- Developing new features
- Improving app performance
- Fixing bugs and technical issues

### Technical Support

- Resolving user-reported issues

### User Communication

- Responding to support inquiries
- Providing assistance with app usage

## Information Sharing

Novelty does NOT share your information with third parties, except in the following cases:

### With Your Consent

If you explicitly agree to share information (e.g., sending crash reports for debugging).

### Legal Obligations

If required by law to disclose information to authorities.

### Protection of Life or Property

If necessary to protect someone's life, body, or property, and obtaining consent is difficult.

<Warning>
Novelty does NOT sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing or advertising purposes. Ever.
</Warning>

## Data Storage and Security

Your privacy and data security are paramount.

### Local Storage

- **Reading History**: Stored in a secure SQLite database on your device
- **Cached Episodes**: Stored in app-specific storage, inaccessible to other apps
- **Settings**: Stored in platform-specific secure storage

### Security Measures

Novelty implements reasonable technical and administrative measures to protect your information:

- **Encryption**: Sensitive data is encrypted at rest (platform-dependent)
- **Access Control**: Only Novelty can access its data storage
- **No Cloud Sync**: No automatic transmission of data to cloud servers
- **Secure API Calls**: HTTPS for all network requests

### Data Retention

Your data remains on your device until:

- You manually delete it (clear history, remove from library, etc.)
- You uninstall the app (all local data is deleted)
- You export a backup (data is under your control)

## Your Rights

You have full control over your data.

### Access Your Data

- **View History**: Check your reading history in the app
- **Export Backup**: Export your complete database to inspect all stored data
- **SQLite Format**: Backups are standard SQLite databases you can open with any SQLite browser

### Delete Your Data

- **Clear History**: Delete reading history from the app settings
- **Remove from Library**: Remove novels from your library
- **Clear Cache**: Clear cached episodes (feature coming soon)
- **Uninstall App**: Complete data deletion by uninstalling Novelty

### Control Collection

- **Incognito Mode**: Read without saving history
- **Disable Crash Reports**: Opt out of automatic crash reporting (if applicable)
- **Deny Permissions**: Refuse file access (limits backup/restore functionality)

## Permissions

Novelty requests minimal permissions to function.

### File Access Permission

**When Requested**: Only when you use backup/restore features

**Purpose**: 
- Export database backups to your chosen location
- Import database backups from your storage

**Required**: Yes, for backup and restore functionality

<Tip>
You can use Novelty without granting file access. You simply won't be able to create or restore backups.
</Tip>

### Permissions NOT Requested

Novelty does **NOT** request:

- ❌ Camera
- ❌ Location / GPS
- ❌ Contacts
- ❌ Microphone
- ❌ Phone calls
- ❌ SMS / Messages
- ❌ Bluetooth
- ❌ Calendar
- ❌ Background location

<Info>
If Novelty ever requests permissions beyond file access, it's likely a platform requirement (e.g., network state for connectivity detection). We will never request permissions for invasive data collection.
</Info>
