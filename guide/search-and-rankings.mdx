# Search and Rankings

Novelty provides powerful tools to discover new novels through comprehensive rankings and advanced search functionality.

## Rankings

Browse popular novels from 小説家になろう through various ranking categories.

### Accessing Rankings

1. Open the **Explore** tab from the bottom navigation
2. Rankings are displayed by default
3. Scroll through the list to browse popular novels

### Ranking Types

Novelty offers five ranking timeframes:

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Daily">
    **Daily Rankings** show novels that gained the most points in the past 24 hours.
    
    - Best for discovering trending new releases
    - Updates multiple times per day
    - Reflects current reader interest
    - Good for finding what's popular right now
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Weekly">
    **Weekly Rankings** show novels that gained the most points in the past week.
    
    - Balances trending and sustained popularity
    - Updates daily
    - Filters out one-day spikes
    - Good for finding consistently popular recent works
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Monthly">
    **Monthly Rankings** show novels that gained the most points in the current month.
    
    - Shows sustained popularity over weeks
    - Updates daily
    - Highlights novels with growing readership
    - Good for finding series worth investing time in
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Quarterly">
    **Quarterly Rankings** show novels that gained the most points in the past three months.
    
    - Long-term view of popular works
    - Updates daily
    - Reveals novels with lasting appeal
    - Good for finding established popular series
  </Tab>
  
  <Tab title="Cumulative">
    **Cumulative Rankings** (all-time) show novels with the most total points ever.
    
    - All-time most popular novels
    - Includes classic and legendary works
    - Rarely changes at the top
    - Good for finding universally acclaimed novels
  </Tab>
</Tabs>

### Changing Ranking Type

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Ranking Selection">
    Tap the **ranking type dropdown** at the top of the Explore tab (e.g., "Daily", "Weekly").
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Timeframe">
    Choose from:
    - Daily
    - Weekly
    - Monthly
    - Quarterly
    - Cumulative
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Rankings">
    The list updates to show the selected ranking. Your choice is remembered when you return.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filtering Rankings

Refine rankings by genre and status:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Filter Menu">
    Tap the **filter icon** in the top-right corner of the Explore tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Genre (Optional)">
    Choose a specific genre to see only novels in that category:
    - Fantasy
    - Romance
    - Science Fiction
    - Mystery
    - Horror
    - And many more...
    
    Select "All Genres" to see novels from all categories.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Filter by Status (Optional)">
    Toggle **Show Only Ongoing** to:
    - **On**: Show only novels still being updated
    - **Off**: Show all novels (ongoing and completed)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Filters">
    Tap "Apply" to update the ranking list with your filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Combining genre filters with different ranking timeframes is a powerful way to discover novels. For example, try "Daily + Romance + Ongoing" to find the hottest new romance novels being updated right now.
</Tip>

### Ranking Entry Information

Each entry in the ranking shows:

- **Rank Number**: Position in the current ranking
- **Novel Title**: Full title of the work
- **Author Name**: Writer's pen name
- **Synopsis**: Brief description (tap to expand)
- **Tags/Genre**: Category information
- **Status**: Ongoing, completed, or short story
- **Episode Count**: Total number of episodes/chapters
- **Points**: Ranking points (if displayed)

## Search Functionality

Find specific novels using Novelty's full-text search.

### Basic Search

Search by keywords:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    Tap the **search icon** in the Library or Explore tab's app bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Keywords">
    Type your search query:
    - Novel titles
    - Author names
    - Words from synopses
    - Character names (if in cached content)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Results appear as you type. The search looks through:
    - Your library novels
    - Cached episode content
    - Metadata of novels you've interacted with
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Basic search searches only your **local library and cached content**, not the entire 小説家になろう database. To search the full site, use advanced search or rankings with filters.
</Note>

### Advanced Search

Use the advanced search modal for more control:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search Modal">
    From the search interface, tap **Advanced Search** or open the search modal directly.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Keywords (Optional)">
    Type search terms in the keyword field.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Genre Filter">
    Choose one or multiple genres:
    - Fantasy (ファンタジー)
    - Romance (恵愛)
    - Science Fiction (SF)
    - Mystery (推理)
    - Horror (ホラー)
    - Action (アクション)
    - Comedy (コメディ)
    - And more...
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Type Filter">
    Filter by novel type:
    - **Short Story (t)**: One-shot novels
    - **Ongoing Serial (r)**: Novels currently being updated
    - **Completed Serial (er)**: Finished series
    - **All Serials (re)**: Ongoing and completed serials
    - **Short + Completed (ter)**: Short stories and completed serials
    - **All**: Everything
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Execute Search">
    Tap **Search** to apply all filters and see results.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Search Results

Search results display:

- **Novels Section**: Matching novels from your library and cache
- **Episodes Section**: Individual episodes containing your search terms
- **Metadata**: Title, author, genre, and status for each result
- **Snippet**: Text excerpt showing where the search term appears (for episode results)

### Search Tips

**For Best Results**:

1. **Use Multiple Keywords**: Combine terms to narrow results
   - Example: "异世界 魔法" (isekai magic)

2. **Search Author Names**: Find all works by a specific author
   - Example: Search for the author's pen name

3. **Use Genre Filters**: Combine keywords with genre filters
   - Example: "龍" (dragon) + Fantasy genre

4. **Filter by Type**: Focus on completed works or ongoing series
   - Example: "ロボット" (robot) + Completed filter for finished mecha stories

<Info>
Full-text search is powered by SQLite FTS5 (Full-Text Search), providing fast and accurate results even with large libraries and caches.
</Info>

## Discovering Novels

### Finding New Novels to Read

**By Trending**:
1. Check **Daily Rankings** for new trending releases
2. Filter by your favorite genre
3. Enable "Ongoing Only" to find actively updated works

**By Popularity**:
1. Browse **Weekly or Monthly Rankings** for consistently popular works
2. Look at **Cumulative Rankings** for all-time classics
3. Try different genres to explore new territory

**By Similarity**:
1. Note the genre and tags of novels you enjoy
2. Filter rankings by that genre
3. Search for similar keywords or themes

**By Author**:
1. When you find an author you like, search their name
2. Check their author page on 小説家になろう (via external link)
3. Add their other works to your library

### Evaluating Novels Before Reading

Before committing to a long series:

1. **Read the Synopsis**: Get a sense of the story and themes
2. **Check Episode Count**: Understand the series length
3. **Check Status**: Ongoing vs. completed vs. abandoned
4. **Look at Rankings**: See if it's popular in multiple timeframes
5. **Read First Episode**: Start reading to test if you like the style

### Using Rankings Effectively

**Daily Rankings**:
- Check in the morning for overnight updates
- Great for discovering new releases
- High turnover, so favorites may disappear quickly

**Weekly/Monthly Rankings**:
- More stable than daily
- Better indicator of quality
- Good for finding series worth investing time in

**Cumulative Rankings**:
- Start here if you're new to 小説家になろう
- These are proven, popular works
- Less risk of abandoned or low-quality novels

## Genre Guide

Common genres on 小説家になろう:

- **異世界ファンタジー (Isekai Fantasy)**: Transported to another world
- **ハイファンタジー (High Fantasy)**: Traditional fantasy settings

- **推理 (Mystery)**: Detective and mystery stories
- **ホラー (Horror)**: Horror and suspense

- **アクション (Action)**: Action and adventure
- **コメディ (Comedy)**: Humorous stories

## Troubleshooting

### Rankings Not Loading

- Check your internet connection
- Rankings require a network connection to fetch from 小説家になろう
- Try switching ranking types or refreshing
- Restart the app if issues persist

### Search Returns No Results

- Verify you have novels in your library
- Check that keywords are spelled correctly
- Try broader search terms
- Remember: Basic search only searches local content
- Use advanced search with genre filters

### Filters Not Working

- Ensure you tapped "Apply" after selecting filters
- Try clearing filters and reapplying
- Restart the app if filters don't respond

### Slow Search Performance

- Large libraries may take a moment to search
- Episode content searches are slower than metadata searches
- Consider using genre filters to narrow results
- Full-text search is optimized but still requires processing time
