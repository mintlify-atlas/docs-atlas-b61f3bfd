# Reading Novels

Novelty provides a rich, customizable reading experience optimized for Japanese web novels from 小説家になろう.

## Opening a Novel

There are several ways to start reading:

### From Novel Details

1. Navigate to a novel's details page from rankings, search, or your library
2. Tap any episode title in the table of contents
3. The episode content loads and you can start reading

### From Reading History

1. Open the **History** tab from the bottom navigation
2. Find the novel you were reading
3. Tap "Continue Reading" to resume from your last episode

### From Library

1. Open the **Library** tab
2. Tap any bookmarked novel
3. Select an episode from the table of contents

## Reading Modes

Novelty supports both traditional Japanese vertical text and modern horizontal text.

### Vertical Text (Tategaki)

Vertical text displays characters from top to bottom, with columns flowing right to left:

- **Authentic Experience**: Traditional Japanese novel layout
- **Ruby Support**: Furigana appears to the right of base text
- **Vertical Scrolling**: Swipe up/down to navigate between episodes
- **Optimized Layout**: Text reflows naturally in vertical orientation

### Horizontal Text

Horizontal text displays characters left to right:

- **Familiar Layout**: Standard horizontal reading for wider screens
- **Ruby Support**: Furigana appears above base text
- **Horizontal Scrolling**: Swipe left/right to navigate between episodes
- **Wide Screen**: Works well on tablets and desktop displays

### Switching Reading Direction

To change between vertical and horizontal text:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Reader Settings">
    Tap **More** > **Reader Settings** from the bottom navigation.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Reading Direction">
    Find the **Vertical Text** toggle and switch it on or off:
    - **On**: Vertical text (tategaki)
    - **Off**: Horizontal text
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Return to Reading">
    Navigate back to your novel. The reading direction changes immediately without losing your place.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Try both modes to see which you prefer. Many readers find vertical text more immersive for Japanese novels, while horizontal text can be more comfortable on larger screens.
</Tip>

## Episode Navigation

Novelty makes it easy to move between episodes without interrupting your reading flow.

### Swipe Navigation

The fastest way to navigate:

- **Vertical Mode**: Swipe **up** or **down** to move to the next/previous episode
- **Horizontal Mode**: Swipe **left** or **right** to move to the next/previous episode

### Automatic Pre-loading

For seamless reading:

- The previous and next episodes are pre-loaded in the background
- Swiping to adjacent episodes is instant with no loading delay
- Episodes beyond adjacent ones load on-demand when you reach them

### Episode Titles

The app bar shows your current position:

- **Short Stories**: Displays the novel title
- **Serial Novels**: Shows "Episode X: [Subtitle]" format
- **Title Bar**: Always visible so you know where you are

## Ruby (Furigana) Display

Ruby text provides pronunciation guides for kanji:

### Enabling Ruby

By default, ruby is enabled and displayed:

- **Vertical Mode**: Ruby appears to the right of base text
- **Horizontal Mode**: Ruby appears above base text
- **Proper Spacing**: Line height is automatically adjusted to prevent overlap

### Disabling Ruby

If you want more compact text:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Reader Settings">
    Navigate to **More** > **Reader Settings**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Ruby Display">
    Turn off the **Display Ruby** toggle.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Adjust Line Height (Optional)">
    With ruby disabled, you can reduce line height below 1.3x for more compact text.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
When ruby is enabled, the minimum line height is automatically set to 1.3x to prevent furigana from overlapping with the line above. Disable ruby to use tighter line spacing.
</Note>

## Customizing Text Appearance

Create your perfect reading environment by adjusting text display settings.

### Font Size

Adjust the base text size:

1. Go to **More** > **Reader Settings**
2. Use the **Font Size** slider
3. Range: Small (12pt) to Extra Large (24pt)
4. Default: 16pt

### Line Height

Control spacing between lines:

1. Go to **More** > **Reader Settings**
2. Use the **Line Height** slider
3. Range: 1.0x to 2.5x
4. Default: 1.5x
5. Minimum with ruby: 1.3x
6. Minimum without ruby: 1.0x

<Info>
Line height is the multiplier applied to the font size. For example, 16pt font with 1.5x line height creates 24pt line spacing.
</Info>

### Font Family

Choose your preferred font:

1. Go to **More** > **Reader Settings**
2. Select from available Japanese fonts
3. Default: Noto Sans JP (included with the app)

### Page Flip vs. Scroll

Choose your navigation style:

- **Scroll (Default)**: Continuous scrolling between episodes
- **Page Flip**: Discrete page transitions between episodes

Toggle in **More** > **Reader Settings** > **Page Flip Mode**.

## Reading Without Saving History

Use incognito mode to read without tracking:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Enable Incognito Mode">
    Go to **More** > **Reader Settings** and turn on **Incognito Mode**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read Normally">
    Open and read any novel as usual. Your progress won't be saved to history.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Disable When Done">
    Turn off **Incognito Mode** to resume normal history tracking.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Warning>
In incognito mode, your reading position is not saved. If you close the app or navigate away, you'll need to manually find your place when you return.
</Warning>

## Offline Reading

Novelty is designed for offline reading:

- **Automatic Caching**: Episodes are cached as you read them
- **Offline Access**: Cached episodes are available without internet
- **Network Indicator**: The app shows when you're reading cached content vs. live content
- **Seamless Experience**: No difference in reading experience between online and offline

Learn more in the [Offline Mode Guide](/guide/offline-mode).

## Reading Tips

### Optimal Settings for Different Scenarios

**Mobile Portrait (Vertical Text)**
- Vertical text: On
- Font size: 16-18pt
- Line height: 1.5x
- Ruby: On

**Tablet/Desktop (Horizontal Text)**
- Vertical text: Off
- Font size: 18-20pt
- Line height: 1.8x
- Ruby: On

**Compact/Fast Reading**
- Ruby: Off
- Font size: 14-16pt
- Line height: 1.2x

### Reading Long Series

1. **Add to Library**: Bookmark the novel for easy access
2. **Download Episodes**: Explicitly download all episodes for offline reading
3. **Use History**: Let history tracking remember your place
4. **Check for Updates**: Novelty detects when episodes are revised (改稿)

### Performance Tips

- Very long episodes may take a moment to parse and render
- Pre-loaded episodes ensure smooth navigation between episodes
- Cached episodes load instantly, even with no network connection

## Troubleshooting

### Episode Won't Load

- Check your internet connection
- Verify the novel is available on 小説家になろう
- Try refreshing by navigating away and back
- Check if the episode was deleted by the author

### Ruby Text Overlapping

- Ensure ruby display is enabled in reader settings
- Increase line height to at least 1.3x
- If ruby is disabled but still appears, restart the app

### Navigation Not Working

- Ensure you're swiping in the correct direction (vertical/horizontal based on mode)
- Check if you're at the first or last episode (no previous/next)
- Try tapping the back button and re-entering the episode
