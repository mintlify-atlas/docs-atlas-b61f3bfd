# Offline Mode

Novelty is designed with an **offline-first** philosophy, ensuring you can read your novels anytime, anywhere, even without an internet connection.

## How Offline Mode Works

Novelty uses a two-tier approach to offline access:

1. **Automatic Episode Caching**: Episodes are cached automatically as you read them
2. **Explicit Novel Downloads**: You can explicitly download entire novels for guaranteed offline access

Both methods use the same underlying storage system, providing a seamless reading experience.

## Automatic Episode Caching

Novelty caches episodes transparently in the background.

### How It Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="You Open an Episode">
    When you tap an episode to read it, Novelty fetches the content from 小説家になろう.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Content is Parsed">
    The HTML content is parsed into structured JSON format for efficient storage and rendering.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Episode is Cached">
    The parsed content is automatically saved to your local database.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Future Access is Instant">
    Next time you open that episode, it loads from cache instantly - no network required.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Benefits of Automatic Caching

- **Transparent**: No action required from you
- **Fast**: Subsequent reads are instant
- **Offline-Ready**: Read cached episodes without internet
- **Smart**: Only caches what you actually read
- **Storage-Efficient**: No unnecessary content is downloaded

### Cache Behavior

**What Gets Cached**:
- Episode content (text, ruby annotations, formatting)
- Episode metadata (title, publication date, revision date)
- Associated novel information (title, author)

**What Doesn't Get Cached**:
- Images (if any)
- External links
- Author comments (may vary)

<Info>
Cached episodes persist until you explicitly clear them or reinstall the app. They survive app restarts and device reboots.
</Info>

## Explicit Novel Downloads

Download entire novels for guaranteed offline access.

### When to Use Explicit Downloads

- **Traveling**: Download novels before a flight or road trip
- **Limited Data**: Save mobile data by downloading on Wi-Fi
- **Unreliable Connection**: Ensure access in areas with poor connectivity
- **Long Series**: Download all episodes of a series you're actively reading
- **Archived Works**: Preserve novels that might be deleted by authors

### How to Download a Novel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Novel Details">
    Navigate to the novel's details page from your library or search results.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Download Button">
    Tap the **download icon** in the top-right corner of the page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Progress">
    A progress indicator shows:
    - Episodes successfully downloaded
    - Episodes that failed
    - Total episode count
    
    The download continues in the background.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Download Completes">
    When finished, the download icon changes to a **checkmark** indicating all episodes are available offline.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Download States

Novel downloads can be in one of four states:

| State | Icon | Description |
|-------|------|-------------|
| Not Downloaded | Download Icon | No episodes explicitly downloaded (may have cached episodes) |
| In Progress | Progress Indicator | Download is currently active |
| Completed | Checkmark | All episodes successfully downloaded |
| Partial Failure | Warning Icon | Some episodes failed to download |

### Handling Failed Downloads

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check Download Manager">
    Go to **More** > **Download Manager** to see detailed download status.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Identify Failed Episodes">
    The manager shows success and failure counts for each novel.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Retry Download">
    Tap the novel with failures and select **Retry** to attempt downloading failed episodes again.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
Failed downloads are often due to temporary network issues. Wait a few minutes and retry for better results.
</Tip>

## Revision Detection

Novelty detects when authors revise (改稿) episodes and automatically updates your cache.

### How Revision Detection Works

<Steps>
  <Step title="You Open a Novel's Table of Contents">
    When viewing a novel's details, Novelty fetches the latest table of contents.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Revisions are Detected">
    The app compares each episode's revision date with your cached version.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Revised Episodes are Marked">
    Episodes with updates show a **改** indicator in the table of contents.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="You Read the Revised Episode">
    When you open a revised episode, Novelty automatically fetches and caches the updated content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Benefits of Revision Detection

- **Always Up-to-Date**: Get the latest version of episodes
- **Author Fixes**: See corrections and improvements
- **Story Changes**: Don't miss plot or character updates
- **Transparent**: Updates happen automatically when you read

<Note>
Revision detection requires an internet connection to fetch the table of contents. Offline, you'll read the last cached version.
</Note>

## Reading Offline

Once episodes are cached or downloaded, reading offline is seamless:

1. **Open Novelty**: The app works without internet
2. **Navigate Normally**: Browse your library and history
3. **Open Cached Episodes**: They load instantly from local storage
4. **Network Indicator**: The app indicates when you're reading cached content

### What Works Offline

- Reading all cached/downloaded episodes
- Navigating between cached episodes
- Viewing cached novel details
- Browsing your library
- Viewing reading history
- Adjusting reader settings
- Backup/restore operations (to local files)

### What Requires Internet

- Fetching new episodes
- Viewing rankings
- Searching the full 小説家になろう catalog
- Updating novel metadata
- Detecting episode revisions
- Adding new novels to library
- Downloading novels explicitly

## Managing Storage

Monitor and manage cached content to control storage usage.

### Viewing Storage Usage

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Data Storage Settings">
    Go to **More** > **Data Storage**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Database Size">
    See the total size of your database, which includes:
    - Library metadata
    - Reading history
    - Cached episodes
    - Downloaded episodes
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Review Download Manager">
    Go to **Download Manager** to see which novels contribute most to storage usage.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Storage Considerations

**Average Episode Size**:
- Short episode: 5-20 KB
- Average episode: 20-50 KB
- Long episode: 50-200 KB

**Typical Novel Size**:
- Short story: < 100 KB
- Small series (10-50 episodes): 200 KB - 2 MB
- Medium series (50-200 episodes): 2 MB - 10 MB
- Large series (200-500 episodes): 10 MB - 25 MB
- Very large series (500+ episodes): 25 MB+

<Info>
Novelty stores content as structured JSON, which is very space-efficient. Even large libraries typically use less than 100 MB of storage.
</Info>

### Clearing Cache (Future Feature)

The ability to selectively clear cached episodes is planned for a future update:

- Clear cache for specific novels
- Clear all cache at once
- Clear old/unread cached episodes
- Keep explicitly downloaded novels

## Offline Best Practices

### Before Going Offline

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download Active Series">
    Explicitly download any novels you're currently reading.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Novel Details">
    View the table of contents for novels you plan to read to ensure latest metadata is cached.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Check Download Status">
    Verify downloads completed successfully in the Download Manager.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Create a Backup">
    Export a database backup to ensure your data is safe.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### While Offline

- Read cached episodes normally
- Use the History tab to find novels you've been reading
- Adjust reader settings as needed
- Take notes of novels to download when back online

### When Back Online

- Open novel details pages to check for new episodes
- Look for **改** indicators for revised episodes
- Re-read revised episodes to get updated content
- Download new releases or additional novels

## Connectivity Detection

Novelty automatically detects your network status:

- **Online**: Full functionality, can fetch new content
- **Offline**: Limited to cached content, clear indicators shown
- **Connection Issues**: Error messages guide you to cached content

### Network Error Handling

When network requests fail:

1. Clear error messages explain the issue
2. App suggests reading cached content instead
3. Retry options appear when connection might be restored
4. Graceful degradation to offline functionality

## Troubleshooting

### Episode Won't Cache

- Check available storage space on your device
- Verify the episode loaded successfully (no network errors)
- Try re-opening the episode
- Restart the app and try again

### Downloaded Novel Missing Episodes

- Check Download Manager for failure count
- Verify failures and retry download
- Check if episodes were deleted by the author
- Ensure stable internet connection for downloads

### Cache Taking Too Much Space

- Review Download Manager to identify large novels
- Remove novels from library if no longer reading them
- Use More > Data Storage > Clear Cache to free up space
- Export backup and reinstall app to clear all data (last resort)

### Revision Not Detected

- Ensure you're online when viewing table of contents
- Close and reopen novel details to refresh
- Check if the author actually revised the episode (on 小説家になろう site)
- Verify episode shows **改** indicator on the website

### Reading Old Version of Episode

- Check if **改** indicator appears in table of contents
- Close and reopen the episode to trigger re-fetch
- Verify you're online
- Use More > Data Storage > Clear Cache to clear all cached content, then reopen
