# Library Management

Your personal library is where you organize and access your favorite novels. Novelty stores all library data locally on your device for fast access and privacy.

## Adding Novels to Your Library

Bookmark novels for quick access:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find a Novel">
    Discover novels through:
    - Rankings in the **Explore** tab
    - Search functionality
    - Direct Ncode entry
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Open Novel Details">
    Tap on the novel title to view its details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Library">
    Tap the **bookmark icon** in the top-right corner. The icon fills in to indicate the novel is in your library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
You can add any novel to your library, even if you haven't read it yet. This is useful for creating a "to-read" list.
</Tip>

## Viewing Your Library

Access your library from the bottom navigation:

1. Tap the **Library** tab
2. See all your bookmarked novels in a scrollable list
3. Each entry shows:
   - Novel title
   - Author name
   - Episode count and completion status
   - Last update date
   - Genre

### Library Information Display

Each library entry provides key information at a glance:

- **Title**: Full novel title
- **Author**: Writer's pen name
- **Status Badge**: 
  - "Ongoing" for serialized novels still being written
  - "Completed" for finished series
  - "Short Story" for one-shot novels
- **Episode Count**: Total number of episodes/chapters
- **Genre**: Novel's primary genre

## Filtering Your Library

Narrow down your library with filters:

### Filter by Genre

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Filter Menu">
    Tap the **filter icon** in the top-right corner of the Library tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Select Genre">
    Choose a specific genre from the list, or select "All Genres" to see everything.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Filter">
    Tap "Apply" to filter your library. Only novels in the selected genre are shown.
  </Step>
</Steps>

### Filter by Status

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Filter Menu">
    Tap the **filter icon** in the Library tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Toggle Ongoing Only">
    Turn on **Show Only Ongoing** to see only novels that are still being updated.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Filter">
    Tap "Apply". Completed novels and short stories are hidden.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Filters are preserved when you navigate away from the Library tab and return. Clear filters by selecting "All Genres" and turning off "Show Only Ongoing".
</Info>

## Searching Within Your Library

Find specific novels in your library:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    Tap the **search icon** in the Library tab's app bar.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Search Terms">
    Type keywords to search by:
    - Novel title
    - Author name
    - Synopsis text (if cached)
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Novelty uses full-text search to find matches within your library and displays matching novels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Removing Novels from Library

Remove novels you no longer want to track:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Novel Details">
    Tap on the novel in your library to open its details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Remove from Library">
    Tap the **filled bookmark icon** in the top-right corner. The icon becomes hollow, and the novel is removed from your library.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
Removing a novel from your library does NOT delete:
- Cached episodes
- Downloaded episodes
- Reading history

The novel data remains on your device. Only the library bookmark is removed.
</Note>

## Library Novel Details

Tapping a library novel opens its details page:

### Information Available

- **Title and Author**: Novel identification
- **Synopsis**: Full story description
- **Metadata**:
  - Genre
  - Status (ongoing, completed, short story)
  - Total episodes
  - Last updated date
  - Word count (if available)
- **Table of Contents**: Complete episode list with:
  - Episode numbers
  - Episode subtitles
  - Publication dates
  - Revision indicators (改) for episodes that have been updated

### Actions from Details Page

- **Start Reading**: Tap any episode to begin reading
- **Download Novel**: Tap the download icon to save all episodes offline
- **Remove from Library**: Tap the bookmark icon to remove
- **Share**: Share the novel with others (if available)

## Novel Update Tracking

Novelty helps you keep track of updates to novels in your library:

### Checking for New Episodes

When you open a novel's details page:

1. Novelty fetches the latest table of contents from 小説家になろう
2. The episode list updates to show any new episodes
3. New episodes appear at the bottom of the list (or top, depending on sort order)

### Revision Detection

When an author revises an episode:

- The episode shows a **改** (revision) indicator in the table of contents
- The revision date appears next to the original publication date
- Reading a revised episode automatically fetches the updated content

<Info>
Novelty detects revisions by comparing cached episode metadata with the latest table of contents. Revised episodes are automatically re-downloaded when you open them.
</Info>

## Managing Downloaded Novels

See which novels are fully downloaded:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Download Manager">
    Go to **More** > **Download Manager**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Downloaded Novels">
    See a list of all novels with cached or downloaded episodes, including:
    - Success count: Episodes successfully cached/downloaded
    - Failure count: Episodes that failed to download
    - Total episodes: Total episode count
    - Download status: Not downloaded, in progress, completed, or partial failure
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Manage Downloads">
    - **Retry Failures**: Tap a novel with failed downloads to retry
    - **View Details**: See which specific episodes are cached
    - **Delete Cached Content**: Remove downloaded episodes to free space (coming soon)
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Library Organization Tips

### For Large Libraries

1. **Use Genre Filters**: Keep track of different types of novels separately
2. **Filter by Status**: Focus on ongoing novels when checking for updates
3. **Use Search**: Quickly find specific titles or authors
4. **Regular Cleanup**: Remove novels you've finished or abandoned

### For Reading Multiple Series

1. **Check History Tab**: See all novels you're currently reading
2. **Download Actively Reading Novels**: Ensure offline access to ongoing series
3. **Use Continue Reading**: Jump back into novels from the History tab

### For Update Tracking

1. **Filter Ongoing Only**: See which novels are still being updated
2. **Check Details Regularly**: Open novel details to fetch latest episode lists
3. **Watch for 改 Indicators**: Re-read revised episodes for story changes

## Library Data Storage

Your library is stored locally:

- **Metadata**: Novel information (title, author, synopsis, etc.) is cached in a local SQLite database
- **Episode Data**: Cached and downloaded episodes are stored separately
- **No Cloud Sync**: Library data is not synced to any cloud service
- **Backup Recommended**: Regularly back up your library to prevent data loss

See the [Backup & Restore Guide](/data/backup-restore) for details on protecting your library data.

## Troubleshooting

### Novel Won't Add to Library

- Check your internet connection
- Verify the novel exists on 小説家になろう
- Restart the app and try again
- Check if the novel was deleted by the author

### Library Not Showing Novels

- Check active filters (genre, status)
- Try clearing all filters
- Verify novels weren't accidentally removed
- Restart the app

### Table of Contents Not Updating

- Pull down to refresh (if supported)
- Close and reopen the novel details page
- Check your internet connection
- The author may not have published new episodes

### Download Status Incorrect

- Open the Download Manager to see detailed status
- Retry failed downloads
- Check available storage space
- Verify internet connection for in-progress downloads
