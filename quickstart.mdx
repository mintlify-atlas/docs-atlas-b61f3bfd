# Quick Start Guide

This guide will take you from a fresh installation to reading your first novel in just a few minutes.

## Prerequisites

Make sure you have Novelty installed on your device. If not, see the [Installation Guide](/installation).

## Your First Novel

<Steps>
  <Step title="Launch Novelty">
    Open the Novelty app on your device. You'll see the main screen with tabs at the bottom:
    
    - **Explore**: Browse rankings and discover new novels
    - **Library**: View your bookmarked novels
    - **History**: See your reading history
    - **More**: Access settings and additional features
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Browse Rankings">
    Tap the **Explore** tab to view popular novels. You'll see:
    
    - Daily, weekly, monthly, quarterly, and cumulative rankings
    - Option to filter by genre
    - Option to filter by novel status (ongoing only, or all novels)
    
    Scroll through the list to find something interesting.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Novel Details">
    Tap on any novel title to view its details page. Here you'll see:
    
    - Novel title and author
    - Synopsis and description
    - Genre and tags
    - Episode count and completion status
    - Table of contents with all episodes
    - Download button for offline reading
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Add to Library">
    If you like the novel, tap the **bookmark icon** in the top-right corner to add it to your library. This makes it easy to access later from the Library tab.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Start Reading">
    Tap on any episode title in the table of contents to start reading. The episode content will load, and you'll see:
    
    - Episode title in the app bar
    - Novel content with proper formatting and ruby text
    - Smooth scrolling or page-flipping navigation
    
    <Tip>
    Swipe vertically or horizontally to navigate to the next or previous episode without returning to the table of contents.
    </Tip>
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Customize Your Reading Experience">
    While reading, tap the back button to return, then go to **More** > **Reader Settings** to customize:
    
    - **Reading Direction**: Switch between vertical (tategaki) and horizontal text
    - **Font Size**: Adjust from small to extra-large
    - **Line Height**: Control spacing between lines
    - **Ruby Display**: Toggle furigana on or off
    - **Page Flip**: Choose between page-flipping and scrolling
    
    Your settings are saved automatically and apply to all novels.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Using Search

Want to find a specific novel or author?

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Search">
    From the **Explore** or **Library** tab, tap the **search icon** in the top-right corner.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Enter Search Terms">
    Type your search query:
    
    - Novel title
    - Author name
    - Keywords from the synopsis
    
    The app uses full-text search to find matches in your library and downloaded content.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Apply Filters (Optional)">
    Use the advanced search modal to filter by:
    
    - **Genre**: Fantasy, romance, science fiction, etc.
    - **Type**: Short stories, ongoing serials, completed serials, or all
    
    Tap "Search" to apply filters.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="View Results">
    Browse the search results and tap any novel to view details or start reading.
  </Step>
</Steps>

## Downloading for Offline Reading

Novelty automatically caches episodes as you read them, but you can also explicitly download entire novels:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Novel Details">
    Navigate to the novel you want to download from your library or search results.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Tap Download Button">
    Tap the **download icon** in the top-right corner of the novel details page.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Monitor Progress">
    A progress indicator shows:
    
    - Number of episodes successfully downloaded
    - Number of episodes failed
    - Total episode count
    
    The download continues in the background. You can leave the page or use other features while downloading.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Read Offline">
    Once downloaded, you can read the novel anytime without an internet connection. Downloaded episodes are marked with a checkmark in the table of contents.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Info>
Downloads are resumable. If a download fails or is interrupted, you can restart it and only missing episodes will be fetched.
</Info>

## Reading History and Progress

Novelty automatically tracks your reading progress:

- **Automatic History**: Every episode you read is added to your history
- **Resume Reading**: From the **History** tab, tap "Continue Reading" to jump back to where you left off
- **Progress Tracking**: The last episode you read is saved for each novel
- **Incognito Mode**: Enable from **More** > **Reader Settings** to read without saving history

## Managing Your Library

Organize your novel collection:

- **View Library**: Tap the **Library** tab to see all bookmarked novels
- **Filter by Genre**: Use the filter icon to show only specific genres
- **Filter by Status**: Show only ongoing novels or all novels
- **Remove from Library**: Tap the bookmark icon again to remove a novel from your library

<Note>
Removing a novel from your library does not delete cached episodes. Your reading history is also preserved.
</Note>

## Backup Your Data

Protect your library and reading history:

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Settings">
    Tap **More** > **Data Storage**.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Export Backup">
    Tap **Export Database** and choose a save location on your device.
  </Step>
  
  <Step title="Save Backup File">
    The backup file includes:
    
    - Your library (all bookmarked novels)
    - Reading history
    - Cached and downloaded episodes
    
    Store this file safely, especially before device changes or app reinstalls.
  </Step>
</Steps>

To restore from a backup, use **Import Database** from the same menu.
