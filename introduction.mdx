# Welcome to Novelty

**Modern, simple, and transparent.**

Novelty is a cross-platform novel reader app redesigned from the ground up for reading works from 小説家になろう (Shōsetsuka ni Narō). Built with Flutter, it delivers the best reading experience across iOS, Android, Windows, macOS, and Linux.

  </Card>

## Why Novelty?

Novelty strips away complexity to focus on what matters most: **reading**. While maintaining essential modern features, the app pursues simplicity that lets you immerse yourself in stories.

### Key Features at a Glance

- **Automatic Caching**: Episodes are cached automatically as you read them, providing seamless offline access
- **Explicit Downloads**: Download entire novels with progress tracking for reliable offline reading
- **Reading History**: Your progress is automatically tracked and synced across episodes
- **Full Customization**: Adjust font size, font family, line height, and reading direction to create your perfect reading environment
- **Backup & Restore**: Export and import your library and history data for peace of mind during device changes

## Technology Stack

Novelty is built with modern, robust technologies:

- **Framework**: Flutter
- **Language**: Dart
- **State Management**: Riverpod
- **Routing**: GoRouter
- **Database**: Drift (SQLite)
- **API Client**: Dio
- **CI/CD**: GitHub Actions
